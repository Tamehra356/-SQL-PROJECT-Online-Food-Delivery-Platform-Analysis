📊 Dataset Overview

This comprehensive database schema represents a food delivery system with detailed customer, restaurant, and order management capabilities.

customers: Customer details including personal information and city location for delivery services
restaurants: Restaurant profiles with names, locations, ratings, and cuisine types for customer discovery
dishes: Comprehensive menu items with pricing, dietary information, and restaurant associations
orders: Complete order records linking customers to restaurants with timing and total amounts
order_items: Junction table connecting orders to specific dishes, enabling multiple items per order
